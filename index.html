Hi, I'm Sameer Neupane

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Memphis, specializing in Mobile Health, Wearables, Human-Computer Interaction, and AI. My research focuses on leveraging AI-driven interventions for stress management using wearable data. I have led groundbreaking projects such as the RELIEF Study and the MOODS Study, which analyze the effectiveness of AI-generated interventions in real-world settings.

With extensive experience in machine learning, NLP, and deep learning, I have built AI-driven platforms and custom visualization tools to optimize stress detection and intervention mechanisms.
